About

Becky Taha'Blu, unapologetically Black, queer, and femme, is the Gothic Glamorous Gangster shaking up the status quo. As an Afrocentric dominatrix and tantric practitioner, she's not just about pleasure—she's about power, education, and smashing oppressive systems. Co-organizing SLUTWALK Denver, Becky leads the charge against rape culture, strutting through the streets lavishly underdressed to trample the patriarchy.

  With a flair for the dramatic, Becky is a Vaudeville performer and producer who uses comedy and charisma to cast spells for equity. Her performances are a fusion of ancient wisdom and modern rebellion, challenging audiences to rethink societal norms. As the head Hoe with the Co-Op, a sex-positive collective, she creates spaces where marginalized bodies are celebrated, and healing is revolutionary.

  Becky's activism doesn't stop there. She's a driving force in the Rogue Support Network (RSN) and fighting for decriminalization and the rights of sex workers. As a proud member of Lysistrata she's on the front lines demanding Universal Basic Income and the dismantling of oppressive systems.


If you're in her presence, expect to be challenged, enlightened, and thoroughly entertained. And remember, "Google Me!"
